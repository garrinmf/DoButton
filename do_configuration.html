DoButton

IFTTT Do Button for Pebble

Requirements

DoButton requires an account with IFTTT (ifttt.com). Once signed up it requires that you have the Maker Channel connected and at least one other Channel that is considered a DO Channel. You can find your Secret Key on the Maker Channel page. When setting up Recipes using the Maker Channel you will have to pick an Event Name. That is what is entered below as the 'Event'. The 'Name' below is what is used in the listing of events you can trigger.
